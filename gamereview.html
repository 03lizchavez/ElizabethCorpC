The Good People

I chose "The Good People" because it sounded like might be a game that will have quite a bit of confusion and wondering. There were some parts, mainly in the beginning of the game where it was like okay I get the point but what's the backstory? Not sure if that's me being impatient or that's something other players have experienced themselves. Like with the game, I feel like context should be provided to the characters because it's confusing as to what's going on and can make the player(s) not want to continue trying to figure out a backstory or context of something dragged out. I do wonder if some options are important to click on or will you recieve the same outcome. I think this game was supposed to build up suspense but honestly, with a little more backstory, it might keep the player interested. Extra changes
